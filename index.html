ABOUT US

History

LNF was founded in the early 2000's by Brianna Legros who had a plan to incorporate tree planting with harvesting. Harvesting and forestry has always had negative comments, but we are here to show that there can be some positives. In it's early day's it was mostly focused on suppling tree's for the communities to by and plant. However, over the years it has expanded to a planting business and now partnering with the loggers themselves


Why you should be interested

As we have learned many things over the years, our goal is to continue to educate the public and teach them more about our services and the logging process. We offer many services including, but not limited to, personal tree planting harvest operations in a safe practice with some of the most highly trained and knowledgeable individuals in our area, and also offering support for those looking to expand their sivil culture business and even buying their first time land.
